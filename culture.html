  • Post-Moralism: Why Objective Morality is Absolute Nonsense

    Post-moralism is the philosophical position that game theory is the best way to understand morality. It is similar to moral relativism since it posits that morality can only be perspective-dependent, but it's different since it also embraces the game-theoretic assumptions of selfishness and rationality.

  • What is Morality?
