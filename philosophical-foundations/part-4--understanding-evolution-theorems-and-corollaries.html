2. Affirmation Of The Senses And Empiricism

  1. The senses deliver valid information about reality when they are not impaired (Corollary of Evolution).
  2. All the concepts we know are derived from sensual experiences.
  3. Thus, sensory input is the first step of the reasoning process (the input sub-process).
  4. Evolution Designed how the Senses Work
    1. Although they give us information about reality, the available information and how it’s presented are first and foremost designed to reach the next stage of evolution.
    2. Thus, senses aren’t necessarily designed to be infallible unless it’s necessary for evolutionary fitness.
    3. Likewise, the senses are only designed to give us sufficient information about how reality works so that we can survive long enough to produce and foster offspring.
